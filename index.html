About Me

I have been practising as an advocate for over the past 03 years. I am working closely with the Directorate of Enforcement (ED) for 02 Years in various high-profile and sensitive matters as an associate in the office of Special Public Prosecutor for ED and also representing ED in the Courts of Law while conducting prosecution of the cases investigated by ED in different Hon’ble Courts of the Delhi under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002. My practice is multifaceted, with a primary focus on matters encompassing PMLA, Anti-Corruption Cases, Bank Securities & Fraud Cell issues, Economic Offences, Serious Frauds, Benami Transactions, Arbitration, Land Acquisition Act, RERA, DRT & DRAT, NCLT & NCLAT, and conventional crimes among others.
